Objective of the Scheme

Launched by Government of India to support stressed MSMEs

To provide guarantee coverage to Scheduled Commercial Banks (SCBs) for providing personal loan through banks to the promoters of stressed MSMEs for infusion as equity / quasi equity / sub-debt in the business eligible for restructuring, as per RBI guidelines.

  1. 90% guarantee coverage from CGTMSE
  2. 10% from the concerned promoter(s)

Salient Features of the Scheme

Credit Facility

To the Promoters of the MSME units upto 15% of promoter’s stake or Rs. 75 lakh or outstanding debt availed by the MSME unit, whichever is lower.

Guarantee Coverage

Guarantee coverage Banks for providing subordinate debt support in respect of restructuring of MSMEs. 90% Guarantee coverage from CGTMSE and 10% from the concerned promoter(s).

Eligible MLIs

All Scheduled Commercial Banks.

Eligible Borrower

Promoters of MSME units which are stressed, viz. SMA-2, and NPA accounts as on 30.04.2020 Can become commercially viable as per the assessment of the lending institutions. MSMEs whose accounts have been standard as on 31.03.2018 and have been in regular operations, either as standard accounts, or as NPA accounts during financial year 2018-19 and financial year 2019-20.

Tenure & Moratorium

The tenure of credit facility would be basis the assessment of debt serviceability or for a maximum period of 10 years, whichever is earlier. Maximum moratorium of 7 years on payment of principal amount.

Guarantee Fee / Charge

1.50% per annum on the guaranteed amount on outstanding basis.
